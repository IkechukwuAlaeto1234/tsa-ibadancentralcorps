ABOUT OUR CHURCH

The Salvation Army
Ibadan Central Corps

The Salvation Army is an international Christian church and charitable organization. Our message is based on the Bible; our ministry is motivated by love for God and the needs of humanity.

Established in Ibadan in [year], the Ibadan Central Corps serves as a beacon of hope, faith, and service in our community, extending the love of Christ through various programs, worship services, and outreach initiatives.
