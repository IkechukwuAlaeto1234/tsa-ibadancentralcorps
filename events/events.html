Community Outreach Program

Join us as we serve our community through food distribution, health awareness programs, and sharing God's love with those in need. Be part of making a difference in our neighborhood.

Serving with Love

Our Community Outreach Program is an opportunity to put our faith into action by serving those in our community who need support, care, and encouragement.

Outreach Activities

  • Food package distribution to families in need
  • Free health screening and blood pressure checks
  • Basic vision testing and reading glasses distribution
  • Free haircuts and grooming services
  • Educational materials and school supplies for children
  • Prayer ministry and spiritual counseling
